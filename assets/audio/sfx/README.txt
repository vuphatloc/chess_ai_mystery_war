This folder contains sound effects (SFX) files.

Download free, royalty-free sound effects from Pixabay and rename them to match the filenames below.

File naming convention & Download Links:
  sfx_move.mp3          - Normal piece move (wood click)

  sfx_move_hidden.mp3   - Hidden piece move (heavy wooden thud)

  sfx_capture.mp3       - Piece capture (wood collision + reverb)

  sfx_reveal_strong.mp3 - Reveal strong piece (swoosh + high ting)

  sfx_reveal_weak.mp3   - Reveal weak piece (swoosh + low thud)

  sfx_check.mp3         - Check alert

  sfx_checkmate.mp3     - Checkmate

  sfx_fog_enter.mp3     - Moving into fog / unknown area (whisper)

  sfx_fog_discover.mp3  - Enemy piece discovered from fog (Shing!)

  sfx_fog_attack.mp3    - Attacked from fog (thunder strike)

  sfx_blindfold_hide.mp3 - Pieces disappear (blow out candle)

  sfx_blindfold_click.mp3 - Click on hidden square (glass tap)

  sfx_blindfold_wrong.mp3 - Wrong move in blindfold (static noise)

  sfx_win.mp3           - Victory fanfare

  sfx_lose.mp3          - Defeat (deep cello)

  sfx_gold.mp3          - Gold/coins reward (coins clinking)

  sfx_button.mp3        - UI button click

  sfx_timer_tick.mp3    - Timer critical (heartbeat tick)

Place your actual .mp3 files here with the exact names above.
